1036660 The War Zones The War Zones
1036661 Factional Warfare revolves around two conflicts, one between the Amarr and the Minmatar, and the other between the Caldari and the Gallente. The majority of the actions take place in two areas of low-security space, known as "war zones", each of which is contested by two empires.

The Amarr-Minmatar war zone extends into parts of the Devoid, Bleak Lands, Heimatar and Metropolis regions, while the Caldari-Gallente war zone extends into parts of the Black Rise, Citadel, Essence, Verge Vendor, and Placid regions. The high-security systems on the borders of the war zones are often used as staging areas, as capsuleers fighting for the enemy militia cannot enter them without being attacked by friendly NPC faction navy ships, although many player corporations also use systems inside the war zones themselves as home bases.

The core Factional Warfare gameplay of capturing systems and running missions takes place exclusively within the war zones. 1036705 Advantage Advantage
1036706 Both factions in Factional Warfare can earn advantage points, determining which faction holds the advantage within a given system. The faction with the highest advantage points within a system gains a bonus to Victory Points scored. This bonus is equivalent to the difference between their advantage points and the opposing side's. For example, if the Amarr faction possesses 30% of their potential advantage points, while the Minmatar faction holds 10%, the Amarr faction receives a 20% bonus for any victory points they accrue. Each system has a base advantage score determined by its friendly frontline and command operation systems. Capsuleers can influence this advantage by accomplishing specific objectives within that system, creating a dynamic strategic landscape where achieving objectives significantly helps to determine factional dominance.
