Hoboleaks Ping Instructions

BETA

In order to get notifications when there's a new patch, you have to add a keyword notification for the word HoboleaksAnnouncement to your Slack settings. Don't worry, it takes 10 seconds, and here's how:

  1. Go to your account's notification settings.

  2. Scroll down to the section called "My Keywords" and expand it.

  3. Add the word "HoboleaksAnnouncement" to the box, without quotes! It should look like this.

  4. Click Save.

And that's it, you're done! You should now receive pings whenever the bot announces a new update. Got any questions? Ask in #hoboleaks.
